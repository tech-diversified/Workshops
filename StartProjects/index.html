So you have an idea...

Everyone has ideas for apps

Great! How do we get started?

Step 1: Landsacpe Analysis

Is anybody doing this already?

How is your idea different?

How can you do it better?

Who is your audience?

Share the findings with your client. Discuss.

Step 2: Break it down

Not like that.

Talk about the details of the idea with your client.

Prioritize

Take detailed notes and compile into a document.

Step 3: Create Requirements Document

Turn your notes into an outline.

Describe the project back to your client

Iterate - modify until you and client are happy

Step 4: Create Feature List

Convert the requirements doc into a spreadsheet

Think of every possible button, screen, option in the app

Discuss with the client

Gives you justification for your estimates

Helps clients understand how difficult it is

Step 5: Estimates

Estimating is an art and science

Different types of estimating (poker, solo, min/max)

Use hours, not dollars

Get input from anybody doing the work

Sticker Shock

Step 6: Draft a Proposal

Includes Timeline - when will you start?

When will you finish?

What are the risks?

What do you need from the client to make the project successful?

Negative Scope: What WON'T you do?

Step 7: Draft a Proposal, cont.

How much will it cost?

Who is working on the project?

Costs for travel, hosting, software

How will you get paid? (Time & Materials, Fixed Cost, N.T.E.)

How soon will you get paid? + 10, + 30 ?

Step 8: Phased Approach

Usually, the price is more than expected

Prioritize

Break the project into phases

Define expectations for each phase

Step 9: Implementation

Create mockups/wireframes

Iterate

Get buyoff on the look & feel before you start building

Step 10: Agile Methodology

Show progress every few weeks

Iterate - get client feedback

Re-prioritize as needed
